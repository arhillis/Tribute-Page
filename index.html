King George VI

Beyond The King's Speech

Anyone who has seen The King's Speech remembers the humle Prince Albert who sturggled with a speech impediment and, after seeking help, delivered one of the most famous speeches in British history. However, not very many Americans know the rest of the story. His rise to power came at one of the most turbulent times in Britain's history: the beginning of the Second World War.
